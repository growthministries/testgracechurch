About Us

Rooted in Faith,
Open to All

God of All Ages Church has been serving our community since 1997. For almost 3 decades, we have been a place where people from every walk of life come together to encounter God, build lasting friendships, and make a difference in our community.

We believe the church is at its best when it is accessible, authentic, and deeply rooted in Scripture. Whether you are taking your first steps of faith or have followed Jesus for decades, there is a place for you here.

Worship

Gathering together to honor God with our whole hearts every week.

Community

Building genuine relationships that go beyond Sunday mornings.

Scripture

Grounded in the timeless truth of the Bible, taught clearly each week.

Service

Loving our neighbors through practical acts of generosity and care.
